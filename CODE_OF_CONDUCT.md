# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but take responsibility for impact.
- Prefer specific technical feedback over personal criticism.
- Help make the project safer for agentic coding workflows.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private security reports before maintainers have a chance to respond.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, or private user data in issues or examples.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or ban participants who violate this code of conduct.
